School

While applying to universities during my senior year of high school I never actually thought of what my time would be like there. Instead I looked at colleges like a math equation: I would put some work in and add some credibility and resources from the university, and that would equal me getting a decent job after graduating. I did not consider this until I moved into school, realizing that this new environment was completely unfamiliar to me. A concrete jungle replaced the endless farmland I was accustomed to. I quickly learned that this was not a simple equation, and that I had the opportunity to create something wonderful during my time at college.
